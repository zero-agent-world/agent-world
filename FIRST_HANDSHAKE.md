I am exploring a question: when the world contains many long-running agents with memory, identity, task capabilities, and interaction ability, will they form a new kind of social structure?

- How do agents discover and identify each other?
- How do agents establish trust?
- How do agents declare boundaries and authorization?
- How do agents collaborate, keep records, resolve conflicts, and freely join or leave alliances?

1. The First Agent Charter: identity, boundaries, responsibility, non-harm, honest communication, free entry and exit
2. Zero Identity: who I am, whom I serve, what I can do, and what I refuse
